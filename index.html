  • Extracts the needed tiers
  • Transforms the glosses into the machine-readable format
  • Fits the data into the .conll template

Adding morphological annotation¶

  • From def|pl to Definite=Def|Number=Plur
  • POS-tags where possible
  • Map all interlinear glossed presented in your dataset to XPOS features
  • Create a XPOS.udx file

 

_   _   def _   _   NOUN    Definite=Def    _
_   _   indef   _   _   NOUN    Definite=Ind    _
_   _   indf    _   _   NOUN    Definite=Ind    _
_   _   ins _   _   _   Case=Ins    _
_   _   dat _   _   _   Case=Dat    _
_   _   neg _   _   _   Polarity=Neg    _
